What is Math?

The better question asked is "what is math to you?" What math is to each person depends on what they have been exposed to.

In elementary school, they have been exposed to adding, subtracting, multiplying, and dividing numbers. So to an elementary school student, math may be the manipulation of numbers.

In middle school, people have been exposed to working with variables. So to a middle school student, math may be the manipulation of numbers and variables.

As people get exposed to more math, their view of math broadens. On the top, under the Subjects tabs, you will find a list of topics that this site currently offers. The goal of this site is to broaden your understanding of math. So please enjoy!
